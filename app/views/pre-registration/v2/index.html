Use this service to register before you take a rapid coronavirus (COVID-19) test at a test site.

You can register yourself, or someone else who is taking a test.

Why you need to register

Answer some questions now, so we know who you are when you get to the test site. We can then send you the test results.

When to register

Do this just before you go, in the 24 hours before your test site visit.

You cannot register too early, because we need to know about your current situation, like if you have symptoms.

Registering at home beforehand will save you time, so you do not have to do this at the test site. It takes about 3 minutes.

If you create an account, registration will be even quicker next time, as some of your answers will be saved.

Who can use this service

You can only use this service if:

  • you’re 18 or older
  • you live in England
  • you do not have [coronavirus symptoms](https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/symptoms/)

What you’ll need:

  • a mobile number (for you, or for the person you're registering)
