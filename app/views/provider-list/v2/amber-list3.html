Providers of day 2 and day 8 coronavirus testing for international arrivals

You will be travelling into England from a country on the AMBER list. This means:

  • you must have a negative test within 72 hours prior to travelling
  • you must take a test on or before day 2 for variant surveillance and a test on or after day 8 to check that you do not have COVID-19
  • you must book your day 2 test before travelling
  • you are required to quarantine for 10 days in your own accommodation
Prices shown in this list are indicative based on the standard testing service. COVID test providers may offer discounts or partnerships with travel providers. Check with directly with providers for details.
