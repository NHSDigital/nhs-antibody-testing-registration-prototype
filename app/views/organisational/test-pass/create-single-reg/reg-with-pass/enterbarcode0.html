Enter the outer return box barcode

You'll find the 11-character barcode attached to the plastic tube, biohazard bag and return box inside the test kit.

Choose 1 of these ways to enter the barcode:

  • use the camera on your phone or computer
  • use your handheld scanner
  • enter it manually
What does the barcode look like?
How to use the barcode scanner
  1. Click in the ‘Test kit barcode reference’ box
  2. Scan the barcode using your handheld scanner. This automatically adds it to the box
  3. Click in the ‘Confirm outer return barcode reference’ box
  4. Scan the barcode again using your handheld scanner. This automatically adds it to the box
  5. Click 'Continue'
