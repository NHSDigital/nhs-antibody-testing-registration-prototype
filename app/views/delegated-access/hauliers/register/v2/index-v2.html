If you're a long haul driver or crew, you must have a coronavirus (COVID-19) test every time you enter the UK, even if you're UK-based. A re-usable test code will help to speed up your experience at the test site.

Who can use this service

You can only use this service if you or the people you are applying for are driving a heavy goods vehicle, van or light goods vehicle into the UK.
