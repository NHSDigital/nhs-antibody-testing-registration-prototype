PCR tests are for people who have coronavirus symptoms, or have been asked to test for a pilot, trial, or to confirm a previous test result.

Other ways to get tested

You can get a rapid lateral flow test if you do not have symptoms. These tests are free and you get a result 30 minutes after taking the test. Pick up rapid lateral flow tests from a pharmacy or collection point today.

Find where to pick up a test near you

In England, you can get a collect code to pick up tests from a pharmacy. Get a collect code on GOV.UK (opens in a new tab)
