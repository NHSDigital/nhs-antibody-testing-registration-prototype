Order and pay for coronavirus (COVID-19) tests if you’re travelling to England from amber or green list countries . You need to do this at least 2 days before you arrive in England.

Check if countries are on the red, amber or green list.

An amber travel package of 2 tests costs £210.
A green travel package of 1 test costs £105.

Who this service is for

You can order tests for yourself and up to 3 other people you’re travelling with.

If you're coming from an amber list country, the other people need to be staying in quarantine at the same address as you.

If you’ve been told that you’re exempt from payment, call 0300 222 0000.

If your travel plans change after booking and paying for your tests, you can change your order or get a refund.

What you need before you start

  • quarantine address in England (amber only)
  • passport number
  • flight, ferry or Eurostar number
  • payment card
Start now

Amber travel package

Order this package if you come from or transfer through an amber list country in the 10 days before you enter England. You must quarantine after arrival for 10 days at a location of your choice.

You must take a coronavirus test on day 2 and day 8 (where your day of arrival is day 0.

Green travel package

Order this package if you come from or transfer through a green list country in the 10 days before you enter England. You do not have to quarantine.

You must take a coronavirus test on day 2 (where your day of arrival is day 0).

Getting your test kits

We'll send the test kits to the address you give us. You should receive them in time to take your day 2 test.

Passenger locator form (PLF)

You must complete this form online within 48 hours of the start of your journey to England: https://www.gov.uk/provide-journey-contact-details-before-travel-uk

About the tests

You’ll take a coronavirus PCR test, which is sent to a lab to be checked. The test involves taking a swab of the inside of your nose and the back of your throat, using a long cotton bud.
