Testing services

  • Symptomatic Testing

    For residents experiencing symptoms, you will need to get a PCR test, this is known as symptomatic testing.

  • Asymptomatic Testing

    Find out how to get rapid lateral flow tests for COVID-19 if you do not have symptoms and why twice-weekly testing is important.

  • Antibody Testing

    An antibody test can tell you if it's likely you've had COVID-19 before. It checks if your body has created antibodies to the virus or if these are from the vaccine.

  • International Arrivals

    The entry point to the get a PCR test journey. Subjects are given an indication of test availability before answering a few questions to indicate their eligibility and options for testing.
