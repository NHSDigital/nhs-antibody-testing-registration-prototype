Register a coronavirus (COVID-19) PCR swab test kit for you or someone else. PCR tests are different to rapid lateral flow tests. You must register your kit so we can send your test results to you.

Check what you need

You should only register a home test kit after you've:

  • recieved it
  • found your neatest royal mail priority postbox or booked a courier to collect it
  • got your order confirmation email containing your bold ten character order ID (you recieved this when you ordered your test kit)

If people you live with have recieved a test kit

  • you should recieve their test kit too
  • each person must use the test kit they have registered to recieve the correct results

Get help to register the test

To have a live video call with trained staff, download the free Be My Eyes app for iOS or for Android and go to the 'specialised help' section.
