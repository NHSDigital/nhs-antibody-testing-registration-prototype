Important information if you live in Scotland or Wales
You need to get the test done in the first 5 days of having symptoms.

For example, if it's Thursday today, and your symptoms started on Monday, you are on day 4.

On days 1 to 4, you can get tested at a site or at home. If you’re ordering a home test kit on day 4, do it by 3pm.

On day 5, you need to go to a test site - it’s too late to order a home test kit.

If you’re now on day 6 or more, do not get a test – it’s too late. Follow this advice on self-isolation.
! Warning If you have symptoms, you and everyone you live with should self-isolate immediately and not leave home until you get your test results. You can leave home to get or post a test.
