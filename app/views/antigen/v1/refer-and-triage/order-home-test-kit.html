About the test

  • the home test kit is free
  • it contains a swab test and instructions
  • it’s posted to your home and should arrive the day after you order it
  • it’s important that you return your test as soon as possible after doing it

Who can have it

You can get a test kit for yourself and up to 3 other people you live with who have coronavirus symptoms.

Some people may be asked to get a test even if they do not have symptoms.

In England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland, a child can have a test whatever their age.
