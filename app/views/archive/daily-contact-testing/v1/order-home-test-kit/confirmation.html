What happens next

Your daily contact testing pack will arrive within 2 days.

You will get an order confirmation email soon.

You must continue to self-isolate until you report your first negative test result to the NHS.

If there are any problems or delays, call 119 and the contact centre can help.

What’s in the test pack

The pack contains:

  • 7 rapid swab tests
  • instructions on how to do the test
  • guidace to help you do daily contact testing and report each result to the NHS

Rapid swab tests (sometimes called lateral flow tests) will give you a result in 30 minutes. There is no need to post a sample to a lab.
