You have ordered a coronavirus (COVID-19) antibody home fingerprick blood test.

What happens next

You’ll be sent an order confirmation.

It’s usually sent via text or email.

The test kit should arrive within 5 days.

You do not have to sign for the test kit.

When your test kit arrives

Everything you need to take the test is in the kit.

To get your results send your blood sample for analysis using the pre-paid return packaging. Post your sample in a priority postbox. If you’re not able to safely get to a priority postbox, use your local postbox.

Watch a video on how to collect your blood sample.

If your kit does not arrive

Wait 7 working days and contact us to organise a replacement.

  • England, Wales, Northern Ireland: call 119 (free from moiles and landlines)
  • Scotland: call 0300 303 2713 (charged at your standard network rate)
This helpline is open from 7am to 11pm.
