About the test

  • it's a free fingerprick blood test that you do at home
  • a test kit is posted to your home
  • the kit contains everything you need to do the test
  • you'll get your results a few days later
  • the test checks for coronavirus antibodies (if you have them, it's likely you've had the virus before)

How to order your test kit

Answer some quick questions and enter your contact details to place your order. This will take 5 minutes.

Check that all the answers you gave us are correct. Then just give us your delivery address.

You'll get your test kit in the post within 5 days.

Do the test and return your sample in the post. Follow the instructions in the kit.
