If you have a home test, use the camera on your phone or computer to scan one of the identical barcodes inside your home test kit. You'll either find them attached to the plastic vial, biohazard bag and return box or loose for you to stick on yourself (please read instructions).

These are different from your pre-paid Royal Mail return package barcode on your box.

You can also manually enter the 11 character reference below the barcode.

If you have a Randox test kit, manually enter the 9 or 10 character unique reference number (URN) on the label.
