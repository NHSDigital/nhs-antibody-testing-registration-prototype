What are support or childcare bubbles?
Bubbles are a way for 2 households to support one another.

The aim is to help people who have been cut off from friends and family (a support bubble) or those who need childcare support (a childcare bubble). There are limited and strict rules on when you are allowed to create, leave or join a bubble.
