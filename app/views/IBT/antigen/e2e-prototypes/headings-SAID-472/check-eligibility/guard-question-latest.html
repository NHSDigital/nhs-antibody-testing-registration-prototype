Make sure you're getting the right type of test

{{ pageTitle }}

You are eligible to get a PCR test if you have any of these symptoms, even if they're mild:

  • a high temperature
  • a new, continuous cough
  • you’ve lost your sense of smell or taste or they’ve changed

You may also be eligible if you:

  • have been identified as part of an eligible group
  • have been asked to get a follow-up test
  • need to get a test for someone you live with who has symptoms
! Warning You should only order a PCR test if you are eligible
