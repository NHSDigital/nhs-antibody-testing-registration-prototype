The technology the service uses to check the result guarantees a high level of accuracy and is authorised by the UK health regulator (MHRA).

If you think the service read your result incorrectly, you can report this to the MHRA on the Coronavirus Yellow Card website. On the homepage, enter ‘Lateral flow test - uploaded photograph result’ in the search box and select the ‘Start report’ button.

You must follow the advice below to help stop the spread of the virus.

What you need to do

Take another rapid lateral flow test as soon as possible.

You only need to self-isolate if:

  • you have or get symptoms of coronavirus
  • someone you live with tests positive, or has symptoms and has not been tested yet
  • you've been traced as a contact of someone who tested positive
