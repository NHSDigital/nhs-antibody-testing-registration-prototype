What you need to do

You must take a follow-up test today, or as soon as possible.

This will confirm if you are infectious to others, start contact tracing and enable you to claim financial support if you are eligible.

Follow the instructions you were given to take your follow-up test. If you were not given instructions, book your test below (choose a test site if you can, it’s faster than a home test).

! Warning Until you get further advice you and everyone you live with must self-isolate immediately for 10 days. The 10 days begin the day after your test date. You can leave home for your follow-up test. Care home residents must self-isolate for 14 days.
