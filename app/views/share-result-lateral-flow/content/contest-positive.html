Stay home and self-isolate

You and everyone you live with must stay home and self-isolate immediately for 10 days. This includes the day of your test and the next 10 full days. Care home residents must self-isolate for 14 days.

Read the NHS advice on self isolation. It covers things like: how to self-isolate if you live with others, what to do about work and what support you can get.
