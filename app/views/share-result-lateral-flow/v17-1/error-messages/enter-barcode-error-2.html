There are 2 types of home test kit, one of which is branded Randox.

If you have a Randox test kit, manually enter the 9 or 10 character unique reference number (URN) on the label.

If your test is not Randox, use the camera on your phone or computer to scan one of the identical barcodes inside your test kit. You'll either find them attached to the items in your kit or loose for you to stick on yourself (please read instructions).

If you have a home test kit, these are different from your pre-paid Royal Mail return package barcode on your box.

You can also manually enter the 11 character reference below the barcode.
