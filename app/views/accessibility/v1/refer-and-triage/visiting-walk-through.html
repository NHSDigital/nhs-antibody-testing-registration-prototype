About the test

  • you need to book your test slot as soon as possible
  • you can choose to go by foot, bike or car (parking may not be available on site)
  • everyone should wear a face covering, except children under the age of 3
  • do not go to the site if you’re very unwell or if you’re at high risk from coronavirus - choose a home test instead

What happens at a walk-through site

A staff member will ask if you need any help (for example, a wheelchair ramp, large-print instructions or access to language translations).

You can bring someone from your support bubble to help you.

You can ask for a staff member to guide you as you do the test yourself.

Who can have it

You can get a test for yourself and up to 3 other people you live with who have coronavirus symptoms.

In England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland, a child can have a test whatever their age.

What you'll need to continue

To book your test slot, you'll need:

  • your mobile phone number
  • the name and mobile number of any other people you live with who will be tested
