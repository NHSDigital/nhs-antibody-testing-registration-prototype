When to order a home test

Patients are no longer required to register their tests as this will be done before they receive the test. Couriers will be automatically booked for patients and pick up the test kit 3 days before the procedure date.

Order tests for your patients 7 to 10 days before their hospital procedure. This allows enough time for them to take and return their test kit.

There are 2 ways you can order a test:

  • use our spreadsheet to order tests for up to 100 patients at a time
  • add each person's details one by one using our online form

Check what you need

You'll need these patient details:

  • date of procedure
  • name
  • date of birth
  • gender
  • mobile phone number
  • NHS number
  • delivery address
