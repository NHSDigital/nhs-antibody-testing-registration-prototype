User Journeys

  • Check Eligibility

    The entry point to the get a PCR test journey. Subjects are given an indication of test availability before answering a few questions to indicate their eligibility and options for testing.

    In Development
    Live Service - 22nd April 2022
    Github source
    In Design - Prototype used in UR

  • Appointment Booking

    Subjects choose a walk through or drive through site for their test and receive email confirmation.

    In Development
    Live Service
    Github source
    In Design - Prototype used in UR

  • Home Ordering

    Subjects give their delivery information for their home test kit and receive email confirmation. Their order is fulfilled by Amazon and is usually delivered the next day.

    In Development
    Live Service - 22nd April 2022
    Github source
    In Design - Prototype used in UR

  • Home Registration

    Subjects who order a home test are directed here from an email order confirmation and the paper instructions that come with the test kit and asked to submit an order ID and their test barcode.

    In Development
    Live Service
    Github source
    In Design - Prototype used in UR

  • Lite Registration

    Allows a subject to submit their registration information and a test kit barcode for PCR or LFD together. Used for PCR where there is no appointment or home order placed, as a replacement for capturing subjects details to Global Registration.

    In Development
    Live Service
    Github source
    In Design - Prototype used in UR

  • Results

    Subjects are given their test results by email and text message.
