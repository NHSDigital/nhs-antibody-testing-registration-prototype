You can enter 2 types of tests:

  • a PCR test (a swab test sent to the lab)
  • a lateral flow test (result is displayed on the test kit)
Where to find the test kit barcode

How to find the PCR test barcode

You’ll find the barcode on the test kit’s plastic tube, biohazard bag and return box. You can either scan the barcode or enter the numbers manually.



How to find the lateral flow test barcode and ID number

These are printed on the test strip. You can either scan the barcode or enter the ID number manually.

Choose one of these ways to enter the PCR barcode or the lateral flow test barcode or ID number:

  • use the camera on your phone or computer
  • use your handheld scanner
  • enter it manually

Use camera

Use handheld scanner or enter manually

If you’re using a handheld scanner, click in the box below before scanning the barcode.

How to use the handheld scanner
  1. Click in the ‘Test kit barcode or ID number’ box
  2. Scan the barcode using your handheld scanner. This automatically adds it to the box
  3. Click in the ‘Confirm test kit barcode or ID number’ box
  4. Scan the barcode again using your handheld scanner. This automatically adds it to the box
  5. Click 'Continue'
