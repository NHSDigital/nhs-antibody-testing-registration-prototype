Follow these rules to take a good photo. Make sure:

  • your phone/tablet is flat and steady, directly above the test strip
  • the C and T letters are the right way up and the specimen well (where you squeezed the fluid) is at the bottom
  • the photo is in focus with no shadows or bright lights on it
  • all four corners of the test strip fit into the rectangular outline
Make sure no personal information is in the photo, for example paperwork showing your name, address, date of birth or bank details.
