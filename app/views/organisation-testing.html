Testing services

  • Elective Care

    A service for NHS Trusts to request pre-registered tests to be sent to patients who have upcoming procedures. They can also manage results and cancel test orders.

  • OBT registration

    Services for Care Homes, Schools, Prisons and other org-based use cases. Service enables single and bulk registration of PCR tests and LFTs, along with the reporting of LFT results.

  • Bulk ordering - Org-led testing (OLT)

    Business users can bulk order test kits to be delivered to them via Salesforce, and onboarding of new organisations.
